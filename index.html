Overview

About This App

DONE:Daily is a personal productivity app that helps you plan daily goals, track focus time, and build a healthy wind-down routine. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app stores and how it is used.

By using DONE:Daily, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

Data Storage

What We Store Locally

All data is stored on your device only, using local on-device storage. This includes:

  • Your daily goals, notes, and reflections
  • Focus session durations and times
  • Work schedule and rest day preferences
  • Recurring goal templates
  • App settings and notification preferences
  • Historical goal data and streaks

This data is private to your device and is never transmitted, uploaded, or shared with any external party — including us.

Permissions

Device Permissions We Request

DONE:Daily requests the following Android permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to send local reminders (morning, work end, weekly). All notifications are generated on-device; no notification content is sent to a server.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM — to deliver reminders at the exact times you set.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — to reschedule your notifications after your device restarts.
  • VIBRATE — to vibrate your device for focus timer completion alerts.

The app does not request internet access, location, camera, microphone, contacts, or any other sensitive permissions.

Third Parties

Third-Party Services

DONE:Daily does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or data brokers. There are no SDKs in this app that collect or transmit your data.

Children

Children's Privacy

DONE:Daily does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since all data remains on your device, there is no risk of children's data being transmitted or accessed by us.

Your Data

Data Deletion

Because all data is stored exclusively on your device, you have full control over it at all times. To delete all app data, simply uninstall DONE:Daily from your device. This permanently removes all locally stored goals, notes, settings, and history.
